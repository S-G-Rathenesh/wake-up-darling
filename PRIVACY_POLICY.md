# Privacy Policy – Wake Up Darling

Last Updated: March 2026

Wake Up Darling respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

- Email address (Firebase Authentication)
- User ID
- Chat messages
- Voice notes
- Media files (images)
- Call session metadata

We DO NOT collect passwords or sensitive financial data.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services Used

### Firebase (Google)
Used for:
- Authentication
- Firestore Database
- Notifications

### Cloudinary
Used for:
- Secure storage of images
- Secure storage of voice messages

Media files are stored securely in Cloudinary cloud storage.

### ZEGOCLOUD
Used for:
- Real-time voice calls
- Real-time video calls

ZEGOCLOUD handles encrypted RTC communication between partners.

---

## 3. One-Time View Media

One-time images:
- Are not stored permanently
- Are not saved to Cloudinary
- Are deleted after viewing
- Screenshot and screen recording prevention is enabled

---

## 4. Data Security

All communication is encrypted.
Cloud services use secure HTTPS protocols.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

Users may delete their account at any time.
All associated data will be permanently removed.
