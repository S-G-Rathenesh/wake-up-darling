# Wake Up Darling Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## Introduction

Wake Up Darling respects user privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

---

## Information Collected

We collect the following information to provide and improve the app experience:

- User account information (email/login)
- Chat messages between partners
- Voice messages sent between partners
- Alarm preferences and wake schedules

---

## Third-Party Services

The app uses secure third-party services for functionality, including:

- **Firebase Authentication** — Secure user login and account management
- **Firebase Firestore Database** — Cloud-based data storage and synchronization
- **Cloudinary** — Media storage for images and voice messages
- **ZEGOCLOUD** — Real-time communication (voice/video calls)

These services may collect technical information required for their operation. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

---

## Data Usage

User data is used only for:

- Partner communication
- Alarm synchronization
- App functionality

We do not use your data for advertising or unrelated purposes.

---

## Data Security

All data is stored securely using encrypted cloud services. Communication between the app and servers uses secure HTTPS protocols.

---

## No Selling of Data

Wake Up Darling **does not sell, rent, or share** personal user data with advertisers or any third parties for commercial purposes.

---

## User Control

Users may delete their account and all associated data at any time from within the app. Upon deletion, all personal data will be permanently removed.
