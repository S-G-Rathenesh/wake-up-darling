# Privacy Policy

**Wake Up Darling**  
**Version:** 2.0.0  
**Platform:** Android  
**Developer:** S.G. Rathenesh  
**Last Updated:** March 2026  

---

## 1. Introduction

Wake Up Darling ("the App") is a couple-based alarm and communication application designed to help paired partners stay connected through shared alarms, real-time messaging, voice and video calls, and private media sharing.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is stored. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address  
- Unique User ID (UID)  

Collected via Firebase Authentication during registration and login.

---

### 2.2 Messages & Communication Data
- Text messages exchanged between paired partners  
- Images, voice notes, and one-time view media  
- Message metadata (timestamps, read/delivery status, reactions)

Messages are accessible only to authenticated paired users.

---

### 2.3 Alarm & Relationship Activity Data
- Alarm schedules  
- Alarm status  
- Wake attempts  
- Streak counts  
- Relationship statistics  

This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore.

---

### 2.4 Call Usage Data

Voice and video calls are powered by ZegoCloud.

We collect limited call metadata:
- Call type (voice/video)  
- Call start time  
- Call duration  
- Connection status  

Voice and video streams are **not recorded or stored** by the App.

---

### 2.5 Media Storage (Local Only)

All media files including:
- Images  
- Voice notes  
- Voice wake recordings  

Are stored locally inside the app’s private internal storage directory.

These files:

- Do NOT appear in the device gallery  
- Are NOT accessible to other apps  
- Are NOT uploaded to external cloud storage  
- Are automatically deleted when the App is uninstalled  

One-time view images:
- Automatically deleted after being viewed  
- Cannot be downloaded or saved  

Users may manually download standard media to their device gallery using the in-app download option.

---

### 2.6 Device & Notification Information
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token  
- Device timezone (used for accurate alarm scheduling)  

We do not collect contacts, call logs, SMS data, or background location.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to:

- Authenticate users  
- Enable partner pairing  
- Deliver alarms and wake requests  
- Enable real-time chat  
- Facilitate voice and video calls  
- Send push notifications  
- Maintain relationship statistics  
- Improve app performance and stability  

We do not sell, rent, or use your data for advertising.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses:

| Service | Purpose |
|----------|----------|
| Firebase (Google) | Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging |
| ZegoCloud | Voice & Video calling infrastructure |

These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- HTTPS/TLS encryption  
- Firestore security rules restricting access  
- Private app-level local storage for media  

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

---

## 6. User Rights

You have the right to:

### Account Deletion
You may request deletion of your account and associated Firestore data.

### Chat Management
You may delete messages for yourself or both partners.

### Data Removal Requests
You may request specific data removal by contacting the developer.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
